AI meeting notes,

MeetMind is a Chrome extension powered by Airia agents. It reads your Google Calendar, monitors live meetings, and automatically generates structured notes, decisions, and action items — without leaving your workflow.

See how it works GitHub ↗
What it does
Everything you need
from every meeting

MeetMind sits in your Chrome toolbar and connects your calendar, meetings, and email into one intelligent loop.

🗓
Calendar awareness
Connects to Google Calendar via MCP. Sees your full day, classifies each meeting type (standup, review, planning), and preps the agent accordingly.
🤖
Airia agent pipeline
5-step Airia agent pipeline: fetch → classify → generate notes → extract actions → deliver. Each step visible to the user in real time.
📋
Structured notes
Every note includes an executive summary, key decisions, action items with owners, and clear next steps — formatted for sharing.
✅
Action item tracker
Action items are extracted with assigned owners and tracked in the extension. Check them off as you complete them.
📧
Email digest via Gmail
One click sends a beautiful HTML digest of open action items to your inbox using the Gmail MCP server.
🔴
Live meeting panel
Injects a floating MeetMind panel into Google Meet, Zoom, and Teams pages during active calls with key point capture.
Workflow
From calendar to action items
in seconds

MeetMind works where you already are — no new apps, no new tabs.

1
Connect Google
Sign in once. MeetMind gets read-only calendar access and send-only Gmail access.
2
See your day
Today's meetings appear instantly — with status badges for live, soon, and done.
3
Generate notes
Click "Generate notes" on any meeting. The Airia agent pipeline runs in ~3 seconds.
4
Act on it
Review structured notes, check off action items, or email a digest to your team.
